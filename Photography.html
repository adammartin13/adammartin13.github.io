Feelings

Spring 2024

Black and white film. Self developed.

This was my first black and white film project and I wanted to experiment with controlled lighting. I wrote some mysterious text with my mouse and a smearing brush in MS Paint, then inverted it so it could be read in the reflection. I placed my camera between one of the displays thats showing the text and an out of frame third monitor thats being used as a key light. I love the mystery, the fuzzy retro feel, and the texture.

Listening?

Spring 2024

Black and white film. Self developed.

I was inspired by a shot from Ingmar Bergman's Persona to create a piece on AI using a mannequin to represent this artificial figure. The individual speaking to someone whose not really there. The lighting and texture around the molded ring is the biproduct of a happy accident as the film was overexposed and the only way to correct was to bring out these super dark shades.

Lincoln Street

Spring 2023
