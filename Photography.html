401k and a House

Fall 2023

Avaricious footsteps carry echoes in the concrete domain complemented with buzzing fluorescent lights. I must look for the exit door somewhere in these cold coarse halls. Salvation is in the structure. There are gaps in the boundaries where the light bleeds in, do not take notice. There is an assurance that my future is within these walls, that if I follow its rules I will find the exit door. Do not go into the light. Trust the structure.
